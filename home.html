Vision

Education is the fulfillment of spiritual responsibilities (Deut. 6:6-7; Proverbs 22:6) with the goal of bringing children to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ, developing in them unimpeachable character through the ssimilartion os Scriptural norms, and properly equipping them with the skills necessary for useful function in life as citizen, and as leaders in their community.

Mission

To provide quality elementary and high school education using a Christian curriculum that produces academically qualified, morally developed, intensely nationalistic, and absolutely God-fearing young men and women who are eager to be trained for definite Christian service.

Pledge to the Christian flag

I pledge allegiance to the Christian flag and to the Savior for whose kingdom it stands. One Savior crucified, risen, and coming again with life and liberty for all who believe.

Pledge to the Bible

I pledge allegiance to the Bible, God's Holy Word, I will make it a light unto my and a lamp unto my path. I will hide its words in my heart that I may not sin against God.
